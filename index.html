Key Learnings

Please see below for three early learnings, with a further deep dive and data analysis taking place at the EOS (SU23: April -June)

01

One Marketing approach requires better alignment between Global/Geo on the Creative to serve Segment 2/C. Late VC deliveries, and limited Present Pack assets meant a less cohesive flow of Past/ Future Pack in the marketplace.

02

Key Colorway supported by marketing engine performed well but ST slowed across the franchise. Media and influencers citing the similarity to (heavily discounted) Pegasus 39.

03

Pegasus needs continue to be integrated into Segment 2 Always On marketing. Geo to influence & inform Pegasus 41 creative approach to connect with target audience.
